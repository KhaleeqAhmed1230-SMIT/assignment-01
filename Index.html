Chicken karahi

"This is a recipe for spicy, flavorful chicken karahi, and it became my favorite because of its rich aroma and homely taste."

Ingrediant:

  • Chicken (1 kg)in 12 pieces
  • Tomatoes( 4-5 medium )
  • ginger paste 1 tsp
  • garlic 1 tsp
  • green chillies 4 - 6
  • chilli flakes 2 tea spoon
  • black paper half tea spoon
  • yogurt 200gm
  • oil 250gm
  • lemon slice ( for serving)
  • Coriander leaves
  • salt to taste
  • cumin seed tea 1/4 spoon
  • dry coriander seed 1/4 tea spoon

Reciepe:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or karahi and add salt
  2. Add chicken pieces and fry with garlic and ginger untill they turn golden brown
  3. Add choped tomatoes and cover it untill then soften and blend it.
  4. Add chilli flakes , black pepper , cumin seed and dry coriander.
  5. still well and cook the chicken untill oil seperate
  6. Add yogury if you want to a thicker gravy
  7. Add green chillies and cook on high flame for few a minutes
  8. Turn off the flame and garnish with coriander leaves and lemon slices

Serve hot it with naan and chapati
